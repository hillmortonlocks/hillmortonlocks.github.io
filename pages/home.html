Meet The Canal and River Trust Volunteer Lock Keepers

We volunteer lock keepers play a crucial role during the busy summer boating season by:-

  • Helping to conserve water by ensuring the locks are used efficiently.
  • Assisting both first time boaters and old hands alike in operating the Gates & Paddles
  • Providing a friendly face and a helping hand along with local knowledge of the area, the canal and its history
  • Undertaking low level maintenance tasks such as painting, litter picking, and gardening
  • To read about the day to day exploits and adventures of Maurice one of our volunteers  be sure to check out his Blog http://www.vollylockie.co.uk/
