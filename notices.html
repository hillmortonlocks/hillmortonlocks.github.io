From 13th of August 2017 until further notice

Due to exceptionally dry weather during the last three months, Hillmorton Locks will be closed overnight.

This will allow backpumps to circulate water more effectively around the canal network.

The locks will be closed between 6.30pm and 9.00am with last entry at 5.00pm.
